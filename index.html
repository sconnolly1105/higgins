Information: Births prior to 1864 were not registered and are therefore unavailable. In such cases, the year of birth has been estimated from the age at death given on the death registration. These estimates are denoted in the tree with the prefix "c.", e.g. c. 1862. Records of births after 1925 are not yet available online; likewise, only deaths registered between 1871 and 1975 are currently available — though this is a moving feast, as more records are released each year.
